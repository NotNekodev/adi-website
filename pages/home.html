What is ADI?

Asriel's Driver Interface (ADI) is a powerful, open-specification driver interface aimed at developers seeking flexibility and performance. With ADI, developers gain access to an interface that's designed for simplicity, performance, and compatibility with both emerging and legacy hardware.
